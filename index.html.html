How it works
I

Create your Forgeborn character

Choose your class — Ironguard, Wayfarer, or Stillmind. Each reflects a real workout style. Your class shapes your abilities, your story, and the world you rebuild.

II

Train in the real world

Complete real workouts. The app syncs with Apple Health and Google Health to verify your sessions automatically. No workout, no XP. That's the rule — and it's the whole point.

III

Level up your character, repair the world

Every session earns XP, upgrades your stats, and advances a serialised story. The more you train, the more powerful you become — and the more of Aethon you restore.

IV

Join a guild. Take on Raid Bosses together.

Form a guild of up to 10 warriors. Weekly Raid Bosses can only be defeated if your whole team keeps training. One member slacks, the team suffers. Real accountability — earned, not forced.
